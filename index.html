Lima

Lima, the capital of Peru, is located on the country's arid Pacific coast. Although its colonial center is preserved, it is a bustling metropolis and one of the largest cities in South America. It is home to the pre-Columbian art collection of the Larco Museum and the National Museum, which traces the history of the ancient civilizations of Peru. The Plaza de Armas and the 16th-century cathedral are the heart of the old Center of Lima.
